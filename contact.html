FAQs

Aksar Pucha Jaane Wale Sawaal

Aapke common questions ke jawab.

Quote lene ke liye kya karna hoga?

Bas hume call karein +91 89558 69034 par ya WhatsApp pe message karein. Aap upar diye form se bhi inquiry bhej sakte hain — hum 24 ghante mein response denge.

Kya aap site visit karte hain?

Haan, Ahmedabad aur aas-paas ke areas mein hum free site visit karte hain. Measurement aur consultation ke baad hi final quote diya jata hai.

Kitna time lagta hai project complete karne mein?

Project ke size par depend karta hai. Generally aluminium windows-doors ka kaam 7-15 din mein aur PVC kitchen ka kaam 10-20 din mein complete ho jata hai.

Kya warranty milti hai?

Haan, hamare har product par standard warranty hoti hai. Material aur installation dono ki guarantee di jati hai. Details project ke time discuss kar lenge.

Payment kaise karna hota hai?

Booking ke time advance, work progress par interim payment aur installation complete hone par final balance. Cash, UPI, bank transfer — sab options available hain.
